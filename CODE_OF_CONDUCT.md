# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, clear, and constructive.

This project welcomes contributions that improve the product, documentation, and developer experience without creating unnecessary conflict or hostility.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and specific in feedback.
- Assume good intent, but be honest about risks and tradeoffs.
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and safety boundaries around local files, secrets, and unpublished projects.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Dismissive or demeaning language
- Sharing private data, secrets, or unpublished project material without permission
- Repeatedly pushing unsafe behavior after maintainers ask for a safer path

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, remove, or reject contributions and community participation that violate this code of conduct.
