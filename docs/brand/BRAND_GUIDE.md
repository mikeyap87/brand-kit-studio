# Brand Kit Studio Brand Guide

Status: Draft foundation

## Brand Summary

Brand Kit Studio is a premium local brand-lab app for turning rough ideas and project inputs into a complete brand source of truth that Codex can keep using.

## Brand Promise

Turn messy early inputs into a project-ready brand package: strategy, voice, visual system, design tokens, starter assets, and future-work rules.

## Positioning

For founders and operators starting many Codex projects, Brand Kit Studio creates a local-first brand package that lives inside the project folder instead of becoming a disconnected mood board.

## Personality

- Precise: show exactly what will be generated and exported.
- Useful: every screen should support a real project setup decision.
- Premium: the app should feel designed, calm, and capable.
- Grounded: do not imply live AI, final approval, or provider output unless connected.

## Visual Identity

- Foundation: light paper-like workspace with sharp ink text.
- Primary colors: deep teal and night ink.
- Signal accents: coral and saffron.
- Shape: 6 to 8px radius, precise borders, restrained shadows.
- Layout: three-zone studio with intake, preview, and export.

## Voice

Speak plainly and operationally. Use terms like source of truth, export pack, project-ready, owner approval, local-first, and design tokens. Avoid hype words like magic, viral, guaranteed, and fully autonomous.

## Future Codex Rule

Future UI, copy, image, social asset, and marketing work should reference this brand guide and the brand tokens unless the owner explicitly asks for a different direction.
